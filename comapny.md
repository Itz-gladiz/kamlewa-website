KAMLEWA Technologies Website: Cahier de Charge

    I. Project Overview and General Specifications

This document outlines the functional and technical requirements for the development of the KAMLEWA Technologies corporate website, designed to serve as the central hub for community engagement, collaboration, and fundraising.

1.  Core Objectives
    Establish a secure and professional digital presence.
    Serve as a model of Cyber Safety and adherence to best security practices.
    Facilitate financial contributions and material donations.
    Showcase organizational impact and future growth plans.

II. Functional Requirements by Page
The website structure will be organized around five main navigational pages, each with distinct functional requirements.

1. Home Page (The Overview)
   Goal: Provide an immediate understanding of KAMLEWA's identity and drive key user actions.
   Identity Display: Prominent display of the Mission ("Foster Technology-driven development...") and Vision ("Cyber Space where everyone feels safe...").
   Dynamic Metrics: Feature a dynamic section showcasing Latest Impact and key numerical achievements (e.g., total youth reached, successful events).
   Primary Calls-to-Action (CTAs): Clear, high-visibility buttons linking to the three main engagement routes:
   Donate (Financial and Material contributions)
   Volunteer (Professional Support)
   Partner (Collaboration Inquiry)
2. About Us Page (The Foundation)
   Goal: Detail the organization's structure, history, and strategic direction.
   Organizational Identity: Detailed explanation of the main operational Pillars (Cybersecurity Advocacy, Digital Inclusion, Knowledge Sharing, etc.).
   Leadership Profiles: Presentation of the President/CEO profile and the core team structure.

Future Plans: Clear outline of growth objectives, including the establishment of regional hubs and niche-based communities. 3. Events & Impact Page (The Portfolio)
Goal: Showcase current programs, communicate impact, and announce upcoming events.
Program Catalog: Comprehensive descriptions of core activities such as STEAM Clubs, Tech Bootcamps, Cybersafety Tours, Startup Incubation, Professional Training programs etc.
Impact Metrics & Gallery: gallery showcasing past events, including photos, event summaries, and key impact data (e.g., percentage of positive feedback, lessons hours delivered).
Upcoming Events: A calendar or list for announcements related to seminars, workshops, and other community outreach. 4. Community & Involvement Page (The Contribution Hub)
Goal: Centralize and simplify all avenues for external support and collaboration
Professional Support (Volunteering): Dedicated sign-up form for individuals offering expertise in technology, education, and cybersecurity.
Strategic Partnerships: Section dedicated to recognizing current partners (e.g., UNDP, ANTIC) and providing a tailored form for new organizational or governmental collaboration inquiries.
Our Communities : An outline of our present running communities and event hosting (KAMCYBER , HACK THE BOX , OWASP).
